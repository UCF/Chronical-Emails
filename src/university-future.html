Colleagues,
The best universities unleash the potential of people and ideas, generating solutions to society’s most significant challenges. At the University of Central Florida (UCF), we are among the top 15 most innovative public universities nationwide and among the top 25 public research universities in student outcomes. This is primarily because of the excellence of our students, faculty, and staff, who are all focused on rising to meet today’s demands and tomorrow’s needs.
You recently received a packet in the mail about UCF’s impressive outcomes in student success, graduate education, research, and industry partnerships (including a sample of our Martian soil simulant). Here are five more reasons why UCF is one of the nation's leading future-focused universities:
Top 25 Public University for Student Outcomes
UCF is redefining what it means to be an institution powering student success. We rank among the top 25 public universities in U.S. News & World Report’s consolidated outcomes rankings. In fact, our consolidated outcomes ranking is equal to that of terrific institutions like the University of Washington, Texas A&M, Ohio State and Virginia Tech.
Faculty Who Reach for the Stars
UCF faculty are innovators, inventors and leaders whose global and national achievements and groundbreaking research and creative works define them as experts in their fields shaping the next generation of talent. And because UCF is America’s Space University, our faculty are leaders in space exploration and probing the cosmos' greatest mysteries.
Orlando's Industry and International Appeal
One of the world’s leading tourist destinations, Orlando is home to UCF’s top-5 hospitality school in the world and a hub for entertainment innovation with the world’s best game design graduate program. We also are the No. 1 supplier of graduates to the aerospace and defense industries and the nation’s top collegiate cybersecurity team thanks to strong partnership with industry leaders like Lockheed Martin and the nation’s leading research centers and companies for modeling and simulation.
An Impressive Rise in the Rankings
UCF is one of only three public universities to improve our U.S. News & World Report overall national ranking each of the past five years. We are Florida’s highest-ranked university for innovation (No. 15), and more than 30 of our graduate programs are ranked among the nation’s best, including 10 graduate engineering rankings in the top 50 for public universities.
Tomorrow's Brightest Talent
UCF is a destination for the nation’s top-performing students, where our commitment to successful outcomes for students of every background has never been stronger. Our Fall 2021 freshmen class had an average SAT score of 1325 and an average high school GPA of 4.25. With 357 enrolled, UCF was ninth in the nation for the number of National Merit Scholars, and we enroll and graduate more Pell-eligible students than the 12 Ivy Plus universities combined.
UCF is building a university for the future that empowers people and their ideas to make a difference in the world while leveraging our location and unique strengths as a public metropolitan research university nationally recognized for innovation, research and social mobility.
