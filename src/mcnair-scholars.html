UCF provides a pathway to diversify higher education through its McNair's Scholars Program.
UCF graduates have gone on to complete or work toward their postgraduate degrees at universities including Stanford, MIT, Georgia Tech, Vanderbilt and many more. Prior to applying for a spot in the program, most of the students didn’t know that a career in academia and research was an avenue available to them.
“I’m not shy about telling everyone that UCF is the gold standard for the McNair program.”
— Anitra Douglas-McCarthy, senior director of recruitment at Cornell

The Impact and Results are Evident
It’s opportunities for students like the McNair program that have improved the quality of research and education around the country while simultaneously propelling UCF among the top 50 public universities for social mobility, graduate indebtedness, and graduation and retention by U.S. News & World Report.
