While first-generation and low-income students are sometimes overlooked, we see future scholars, researchers, entrepreneurs, and doers who need a little support — and we provide it. A significant contribution to that support recently came in a $40 million gift from MacKenzie Scott, who recognized UCF as a high-impact organization. And we plan to double her donation and impact, by inspiring donors to match her $40 million — producing even greater outcomes for our students.
“Higher education is a proven pathway to opportunity”
— MacKenzie Scott
An Inspiring Match
Trusting UCF to spend as it sees fit, this transformational investment will allow UCF to increase our contribution to upward social mobility and change the trajectory of individual lives. To do this, we will leverage Ms. Scott’s gift by doubling the impact of donors who contribute to endowed funds and match the yield of their endowed gift, supporting students and faculty at UCF. To quote Ms. Scott, “Generosity is generative,” and we will show the mission of helping students of all backgrounds achieve a higher education degree can inspire generosity and generate twice the impact.
