Orlando's hometown university is among the best in the nation
UCF is building the university for the future by unleashing the potential of people and ideas to positively impact the world. In doing so, we continue to be recognized as a leader in higher education and this year moved up to No. 64 as a top-ranked public institution in U.S. News & World Report’s 2023 Best Colleges rankings.

This year’s public school ranking is the highest in our history as our university and faculty continue to gain recognition for making an impact in the lives of students, Central Floridians, and people across the globe. This year’s notable increases in U.S. News & World Report rankings highlight our growing national reputation. The university aims to accelerate this trajectory of positive influence, striving for a top 50 ranking among public schools by 2027.
For the fifth year in a row, UCF is a top 20 Most Innovative university, placing us in the same company as excellent universities such as the University of Michigan, Harvard, Georgia Tech, and the University of Texas at Austin. This national ranking is a recognition that we continue to innovate in our classrooms, labs and with our partners across Central Florida and the state.
UCF continues to unleash the potential inherent in every individual, ranking #41 among public universities as a Top Performer in Social Mobility. We will continue to invest in student success and well-being by offering high-quality post-secondary education that aligns with the knowledge and skills needed by the workforce of the future.
