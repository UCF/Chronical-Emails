It’s just the latest acknowledgement of UCF’s achievement in improving student outcomes as 30 graduate programs and areas were recognized in the publication’s 2022 Best Graduate Schools rankings earlier this year.
With top-tier talent and game-changing creativity, UCF’s graduate programs and researchers are dedicated to solving global challenges. Beyond stellar rankings, UCF produces innovators, inventors and leaders who perpetuate the pursuit of knowledge.
  • UCF’s first Gates Cambridge Scholar will conduct stem-cell therapy research in one of world’s most prestigious postgraduate scholarships.
  • Developed by UCF, the world’s first optical oscilloscope measures the electric field of light based on all-solid-state materials.
  • Backed by a $10-million NASA mission, UCF will develop zero-carbon jet engines to revolutionize aviation industry.
  • A UCF graduate led the charge to create Moderna’s COVID-19 vaccine, opening possibilities for future of medicine.
Growing in demand and reputation, UCF’s graduate programs attract exceptional students and faculty from across the globe as a national and international destination for unleashing potential that will change the world.
