With more than 30 graduate programs ranked among the nation’s best by U.S. News & World Report, the University of Central Florida proves itself a leader — yet again — in future-focused academics and innovative research. With 10 graduate engineering rankings in the top 50 for public universities and the nation’s No. 3 ranked program in homeland security and emergency management, plus one of the world’s foremost optical and photonics programs, UCF faculty are producing the talent, research and ideas that build a better future for us all.
