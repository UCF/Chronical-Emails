Since its founding in 1963 with the mission to provide talent for the growing U.S. space program, the University of Central Florida has risen to the challenge of advancing our progress in space — everything from the possibilities of mining the moon to simulating Martian soil to the impact of space dust. Our faculty are industry professionals who contribute to NASA missions while our alumni have also gone on to take giant leaps in the space industry with companies such as Boeing, Lockheed Martin, SpaceX and Blue Origin.
UCF is committed to doing the work today that solves tomorrow’s big questions. As we look to return to the moon, venture to Mars and expand our understanding of our place in the galaxy, one thing is for certain — our faculty, students and alumni will answer the call to turn possibilities into reality.
